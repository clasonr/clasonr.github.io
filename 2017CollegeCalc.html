I want you to note, an 86% means you are college ready. This means that you will get out of a junior college
or finish your general education at a 4 yr school in ~2 years. Any additional time added on means
you'll have to spend more time at a junior college or 4 year school.

Directions: The career you picked has an associated years of college you need. You will need
to add the additional years of college you need to achieve a degree for your chosen career/field.
So if your degree normally takes 8 years, you will need to add the additional years
to the total (example: 8+1 = 9 years). You will need to enter the amount of the yearly cost
for each year in the 'total for the year' column. Of course half a year is half the cost.

Note: Never count on money you do not have, i.e. grants and scholarships.
Therefore, the assumption will be that you will need to take out loans for every year of college.
